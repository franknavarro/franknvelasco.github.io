Color Charge

Stop feeling blue & play Color Charge.

Color Charge is a world of color that shows the potential of the three primary colors and your skills to use them. Charge your way through red, blue, yellow, green, purple, orange and white lines as they pick up speed towards the box of colors. Sounds easy enough, and with eye pleasing colors for a truly colorful experience, Color Charge is simple enough for anyone. Get through the colors and get up a high score to be the bes on the leaderboards. For to charge through the magnificent colors will be an awfully big adventure!
